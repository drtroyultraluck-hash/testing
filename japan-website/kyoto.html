Kyoto

Kyoto is the cultural heart of Japan, where centuries-old temples, traditional wooden houses, and peaceful gardens preserve the country's rich heritage. Unlike the fast-paced energy of Tokyo, Kyoto moves with quiet elegance. It is a city of tea ceremonies, geisha districts, bamboo forests, and breathtaking seasonal beauty — especially during cherry blossom and autumn foliage seasons.
